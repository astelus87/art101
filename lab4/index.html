Lab 4 - Pseudocoding

Challenge

For this lab, we were asked to first practice pseudocoding by making an ordered list of how to accomplish an everyday task. We decided on psuedocoding putting on makeup, since this was a routine that we both did regularly, so we could work on the list together. After that, we worked on task 3 & 4, where we decided on psuedocoding "Rock Paper Scissors".

Problems

We were a little bit confused about what this lab specifically was asking of us, and what exactly we were expected to do in this class. We did our best to interpret the instructions, and worked together to talk out any confusion we had. For some reason, when pasting the psuedocode into the results section with the "pre" & "code" tag, but it resulted in a huge indent gap, so I made it into an unordered list instead to solve that.

Reflection

Overall, I think we did our best with the new concept introduced. It's a bit of a confusing concept, since we don't see the direct result of anything that we are doing, and it doesn't seem to have any tangible function, only conceptual. Because of this, it was a bit difficult to gauge if we were being specific enough with our list, and if not, what we could add more on without overcomplicating it.

Psuedocode - Everyday Task

  1. Prep face with moisturizer and sunscreen, rub the product onto the whole face gently.
  2. Take out eyebrow products, eyeshadow and brush, and fill in brows.
  3. Take out eyeshadow and eyeshadow brush, and brush the product onto eyelids.
  4. Put on eyeliner with eyeliner felt tip brush.
  5. Take out the lipstick product, and fill in the top and bottom lip with the chosen shade.
  6. Take out a big brush, and the blush product. Brush blush on face.
